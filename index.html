Christmas is an annual holiday commemorating the birth of Jesus Christ. Each year it's observed on December 25 by billions of people around the world. Christmas is typically celebrated by exchanging gifts, decorating fir trees, enjoying dinner in the company of loved ones, and hanging large stockings over a hearth or mantle where they get stuffed with small presents for children who are told they were left there by Santa Claus.
